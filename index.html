About Me

My journey has been driven by a desire to uncover insights from data to inform smarter business decisions. I’ve gained hands-on experience through roles such as:

I am skilled in Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and data visualization tools, with experience in machine learning and data mining. My goal is to bridge the gap between data and business strategy, helping organizations achieve their objectives through data-driven insights.

In my free time, I enjoy photography, hiking, and exploring new technologies, always seeking new ways to learn and grow.

I’m excited about opportunities where I can apply my skills and passion to make an impact as a Business Analyst.
